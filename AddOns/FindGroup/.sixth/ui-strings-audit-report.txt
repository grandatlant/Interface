Notes
- Audit limited to explicit user-facing XML text/tooltip literals only.
- Dynamic dropdown labels produced by Lua helpers (e.g. patch names, difficulty names, tooltip bodies from `FindGroup_Tooltip_All`) are out of XML scope and should be handled by the parent or other module audits.
- Some recommended replacements are intentionally concise to fit Wrath-era button widths.
